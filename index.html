What is Al-Kauther water treatment and ultra-violet sterilizer and how does it work?

Ultraviolet rays from the sun have long been known their ability to destroy micro-organisms. However it has only been in recent years that ultraviolet equipment has been manufactured for home consumers. Ultraviolet is the band of light located in the spectrum between 2,000 â€“ 3,000 Angstrom wavelengths. The most effective germicidal wavelength is at 2537 (254NM). Ultraviolet energy at this wavelength scrambles the DNA structure of the micro-organisms and makes them sterile.
